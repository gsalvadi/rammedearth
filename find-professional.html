Find a Professional

Looking for a rammed earth professional in your area? Submit your project details and I'll manually review your request to connect you with the right person from the directory.

This is for finding professionals, not for working with me directly. If you want consultation or tech transfer from me personally, visit Work with me.

How to Submit Your Request

Choose one of the following methods to send your referral request:

Option 1: Email Directly

Send an email to info@rammedearth.in with the following information:

Subject: Professional Referral Request - [Your Location]

Include in your email:

  • Your name
  • Your email
  • Your location (City, State/Region, Country)
  • What you're looking for (Builder, Architect, Consultant, Workshop, etc.)
  • Project details (What are you planning? What stage are you at?)
  • Timeline (when you plan to start)
Open Email Template

Option 2: Download Template

Download a template file, fill in your details, and send it as an attachment to info@rammedearth.in
