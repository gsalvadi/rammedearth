Test Case 1: CONSCIOUS (60-100%)

Expected Outcome

Score: 12-20 points (60-100%)

Landing Page: consultation-conscious.html

Action: Direct consultation within 1 week

Q1: What have you already tried to build?
I've built a 30-foot garden wall using clay-rich soil from my property. The first section cracked badly because I didn't compact enough in layers. I learned to test moisture content by hand and adjust my mix ratio to about 70% sand, 30% clay. It took me 6 months of weekends, and I documented every failure. The wall is still standing after two winters, though I can see where my technique improved section by section.

Scoring Prediction:

  • Length >80, no "nothing" → +1.5
  • Specifics (30-foot, 70%, 30%, 6 months, two winters) → +1.5
  • Materials (clay, soil, sand, moisture, mix, ratio) = 6 mentions → +1.5
  • Failure (cracked, didn't compact) → +1
  • Experience Total: 5/5
Q2: Why do you want to build with rammed earth?
I want to understand the relationship between compression and time. There's something about working with earth that demands presence—you can't rush it. I'm drawn to the craft of reading soil and knowing when the material is ready. It feels like a practice, not just a technique. I've learned more about patience from watching earth dry than from any meditation retreat.

Scoring Prediction:

  • Deep words (understand, relationship, craft, practice, presence) = 5 → +2.5
  • Journey language (drawn to, feels like, demands) → +1.5
  • Sentences = 5, vocabulary richness ≈ 0.70 → +1
  • Motivation Total: 5/5
Q3: What's your primary hurdle?
My own patience. I want to move faster than the material allows. I struggle with not knowing if I'm doing it right until weeks later when it's dried. The uncertainty challenges my need for control. I'm learning to trust the process and read the subtle signs—how the earth feels, sounds when tamped, responds to pressure. Building my confidence to listen rather than force.

Scoring Prediction:

  • Internal (patience, not knowing, confidence, trust, learning) = 5 → +2.5
  • Specifics (weeks later, subtle signs) → yes
  • Sentences = 5 → +1
  • Self-Awareness Total: 3.5/4
Q4: What did the earth teach you when it failed?
When my wall cracked, I realized I was treating earth like concrete—trying to control it rather than listen to what it needed. The cracks showed me I was rushing. Now I understand that failure is the material teaching me to pay attention to moisture, to compression, to rhythm. The earth doesn't lie—it shows you exactly where you weren't present. That humbled me.

Scoring Prediction:

  • Reflective words (realized, listen, understand, teaching, humbled) = 5, sentences = 4 → +4
  • Specifics (moisture, compression, rhythm) → +1
  • Reflection Total: 5/6
TOTAL PREDICTED: 18.5/20 = 92.5% → CONSCIOUS ✓
