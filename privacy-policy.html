Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Simple version: I collect only what's needed to respond to your inquiry. Your email and responses are stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties, except when you explicitly consent to be referred to other professionals in our directory.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on this site, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your country and city/region (for professional matching)
  • Your responses to consultation or workshop questions
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics beyond basic server logs.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your consultation, workshop, or tech transfer inquiry
  • Match you with appropriate professionals in our directory (only with your explicit consent)
  • Send you relevant updates about workshops or rammed earth resources (you can opt out anytime)
  • Improve our assessment process and resources

Referrals to Directory Professionals

When you check the consent box on our forms, you authorize us to:

  • Share your email, location, and inquiry details with professionals in our directory who may be a better match for your needs
  • Notify you when such a referral is made
  • Provide your contact information to the referred professional so they can reach out directly

Referrals are made at our discretion based on location, project needs, and professional availability. Not all inquiries result in referrals.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored in:

  • Google Sheets (secured with industry-standard encryption)
  • Email systems for correspondence

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Active inquiries: Until resolved or you request deletion
  • Workshop registrations: Until the workshop occurs or you unsubscribe
  • Past consultations: Indefinitely for reference, unless you request deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of referrals or communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, email your request and we'll respond within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Workspace - For data storage and email
  • Zapier - For notification automation

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country. By submitting a form, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the main page. Continued use of our forms after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact
