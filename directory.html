Dhruv Chandra Sud
Architect,Builder
The Mythe Guild & Studio is a design + build practice based in Simla, Dharamsala & Dehradun.
Our design philosophy promotes craftsmanship, conservation and ethically/ ecologically responsible development in the lower Himalayas, and tends to focus on
- working with (rather than against) the natural terrain, & ecology of the Himalayas
- using locally sourced natural materials and hand crafted skills to create more enriching architectural & interior experiences
- combining the wisdom & sensibility of pahari building techniques (like adobe, rammed earth, dry stone masonry, dhajji diwari, kath kuni, etc) with the strength and durability of industrial/ prefab possibilities
- providing for contemporary lifestyle, comfort & luxury within the timeless tradition of Himalayan architecture.
Professional commissions have varied from developing organic farms, orchards and eco communities, boutique residences, hospitality & real estate projects, to conservation & adaptive re-use of historic buildings.
