1. Move Quizzes to Dedicated Pages ✅ HIGH PRIORITY
Problem: index.html contains all three quizzes (consultation, tech transfer, workshop), making it massive and slow.

Solution: Create separate pages for each quiz type:

consultation-quiz.html - Self-builder readiness assessment
tech-transfer-quiz.html - Professional capability assessment
workshop-interest.html - Simple interest form
Navigation Flow:

index.html (About tab) → "Work with me" button → quiz-index.html
                                                    ↓
                                     [Choose your path: 3 cards]
                                                    ↓
                        consultation-quiz.html | tech-transfer-quiz.html | workshop-interest.html
Benefits:

Reduces index.html from 98KB to ~30-40KB
Faster page load for first-time visitors
Cleaner analytics (track conversion per quiz type)
Easier A/B testing of quiz questions
Better mobile experience
